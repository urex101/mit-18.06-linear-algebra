## Topics covered

Systems of linear equations and elimination, matrix operations and inverses, LU factorization, vector spaces and the four fundamental subspaces, orthogonality, projections and least squares, determinants, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, diagonalization, symmetric and positive definite matrices, similar matrices, and the singular value decomposition (SVD).

## Why

Building the mathematical foundation for a career in quantitative trading — linear algebra underpins regression, portfolio variance, covariance estimation, and most of the numerical methods used in the field. Studied alongside 14.01/14.02 (economics), 6.041 (probability), and CS50P (Python).
